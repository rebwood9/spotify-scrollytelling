Every song I played, every skip, every 3 a.m. repeat — logged since 2014. Here is what the record says.

Where the data comes from

Data is pulled from Spotify's Extended Streaming History, and includes listening history from 2014 through early 2025. Genre data is added using organizeyourmusic.playlistmachinery.com.

Total Listening Time Across The Years

Across all my listening data, there isn't a clear trend. Generally speaking, I listen to more music while I am in school than out of school. I attended undergrad 2013-2014 and completed a masters 2022-2024.

Montly Listening Time Across The Years

Becoming more granular, we can look at a heat map to see if there are any patterns between years. Perhaps I listen to more music in the summers than the winters? What stands out to me are the two extremes. January of 2015, I was faffing about New Zealand, engaging in a massive "Year of Firsts." October of 2020, I had just moved to Oregon. We don't talk about Oregon. However, I will note that there are three periods of time where I was not a happy camper and those periods line up with the dark patches in this heat map.
